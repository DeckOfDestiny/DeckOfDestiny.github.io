Overview

What players are trying to do

Objective

Play 15 rounds and finish with the most Destiny Points by winning rounds with number cards and using action cards at the right time.

Setup

  • Shuffle the Number Deck and deal 15 number cards to each player.
  • Shuffle the Action Deck and deal 5 action cards to each player.
  • Each player begins with a 20-card hand.
  • Return any leftover cards to the box.
  • Rebalanced deck: low and mid number cards are more common than 11s and 12s.

Game Flow

Every round follows the same 3-step rhythm

1

Choose a Card

Each player secretly chooses 1 or 2 cards and places them face down. If you play 2 cards, one must be a number card and one must be an action card.

2

Reveal

All players flip their chosen cards face up at the same time.

3

Resolve

Resolve action effects by priority, compare final numbers, and award Destiny Points for the round.

Important exception

If a player runs out of number cards before round 15, they may continue by playing only action cards.

Action Cards

Six one-use effects that can flip a round

Boost (+3)

Adds 3 to your number this round.

If played alone, it counts as 0 + 3.

Shield

Protects your card from being altered. Ignore effects that would lower, steal, or swap it.

Swap

Choose another player and swap your revealed number with theirs before scoring.

Double Down

Your card’s value is doubled.

Maximum value after doubling is 18.

Drain (-3)

Choose an opponent and reduce their number by 3.

A reduced value cannot go below 0.

Steal Point

If you do not win the round, steal 1 Destiny Point from the winner, but only if the winner has at least 2 points.

Resolution Order

Action timing matters as much as the card itself

Shields activate first

Protection is established before any lowering or swapping effects can touch a card.

Scoring & Winning

Rounds reward aggression, but ties punish everyone else

Round Winner

The highest final total wins the round and gains 1 Destiny Point.

Tied for First

Players tied for first do not lose a point. Every other player loses 1 Destiny Point if they have one to lose.

End of Game

After 15 rounds, the player with the most Destiny Points wins.

Tiebreakers

  1. Most action cards remaining.
  2. If still tied, the tied players share the victory.
