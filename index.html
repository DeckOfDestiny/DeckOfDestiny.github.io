Objective

Win the most Destiny Points in 15 rounds.

Play number cards efficiently, use action cards for swing moments, and finish the match ahead of the table.

Setup

Each player starts with 20 cards.

  • Deal 15 number cards to each player.
  • Deal 5 action cards to each player.
  • Return any leftovers to the box.
  • Low and mid numbers are more common than 11s and 12s.

Round Flow

Choose, reveal, resolve.

1

Choose

Play 1 or 2 cards. If 2, one must be a number and one must be an action.

2

Reveal

All players flip at the same time.

3

Resolve

Apply effects by priority and score the highest final value.

Actions

Six one-use effects

Boost (+3)

Adds 3 to your number. Played alone, it counts as 0 + 3.

Shield

Protects your card from lower, steal, and swap effects.

Swap

Choose another player and swap revealed numbers before scoring.

Double Down

Double your value, up to a maximum of 18.

Drain (-3)

Reduce an opponent’s number by 3, down to a minimum of 0.

Steal Point

If you do not win, steal 1 point from the winner if they have at least 2.

Priority

Resolution Order

  1. Shields
  2. Drains
  3. Swaps
  4. Doubling and Boosts
  5. Compare numbers and award points
  6. Steal Point resolves last

Winning

Highest score after 15 rounds wins.

  • Tied first place means everyone outside that tie loses 1 point if possible.
  • If you run out of numbers, you may keep playing action cards only.
  • Tiebreaker: most action cards remaining, then shared victory.
